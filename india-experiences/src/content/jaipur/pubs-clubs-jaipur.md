**Jaipur's nightlife centers around C-Scheme area, featuring clubs like Blackout (₹1200-1500 entry), F Bar & Lounge (₹800-1000), and hotel-based venues. All enforce 21+ age limits with entry fees ranging ₹500-1500 ($6-18), varying by weekday versus weekend crowds.**

## Top Clubs in C-Scheme

### Blackout
Located on Ashok Marg, Blackout is Jaipur's premier nightclub. Entry fees range from ₹800 ($10) on weekdays to ₹1500 ($18) on weekends. The club features electronic dance music and Bollywood remixes, with guest DJs performing regularly. Weekend crowds pack the dance floor by 10 PM, while weekdays maintain a more relaxed lounge atmosphere.

### F Bar & Lounge
This two-level venue on C-Scheme combines dining with nightlife. Entry costs ₹600-1000 ($7-12), often waived if you're dining. The ground floor serves as a restaurant until 9 PM, transforming into a club space later. Music ranges from commercial Bollywood to international pop hits.

### Grunge
Situated near Sawai Ram Singh Road, Grunge attracts a younger crowd with its industrial décor and rock-influenced playlist. Entry fees start at ₹500 ($6) on weekdays, reaching ₹1000 ($12) on Saturdays. The venue regularly hosts live music performances and theme nights.

## Hotel-Based Nightlife

### Sky Bar at Hotel Clarks Amer
This rooftop venue offers panoramic city views with entry fees of ₹800-1200 ($10-15). The music leans toward lounge and deep house, attracting a more mature clientele. Weekend bookings are essential as capacity fills quickly.

### Polo Bar at Rambagh Palace
The most upscale option requires smart casual dress and charges ₹1500-2000 ($18-24) entry on weekends. Classical and jazz music dominate the playlist, creating an elegant atmosphere distinct from typical club scenes.

## Music Styles and Crowd Dynamics

Most venues play a mix of Bollywood hits, international pop, and electronic dance music. Weekend crowds arrive after 9:30 PM and peak around 11 PM to 1 AM. Weekday atmospheres remain more conversational, with music volumes lower and seating more available.

C-Scheme venues attract college students and young professionals aged 21-35. Hotel-based clubs draw tourists and older locals seeking sophisticated environments. The gender ratio stays relatively balanced, though weekend crowds skew more heavily male.

## Entry Requirements and Logistics

All venues strictly enforce the 21+ age requirement mandated by Rajasthan state law. Carry photo identification – Aadhaar cards, passports, or driver's licenses are accepted. Many clubs require dress codes prohibiting shorts, flip-flops, or overly casual attire.

Entry fees typically include one complimentary drink, though premium liquor costs extra. Most venues accept cash and UPI payments. Weekend reservations help avoid long queues, especially at popular spots like Blackout.

When planning your [nightlife exploration]((/jaipur/)), consider that Jaipur's club scene operates Thursday through Saturday, with most venues closed Sunday through Wednesday. Transportation options include auto-rickshaws, Uber, and Ola, though booking rides becomes challenging after midnight during peak hours.

## Safety and Transportation

C-Scheme remains well-lit and patrolled, making it relatively safe for nightlife activities. However, travel in groups when possible and arrange transportation in advance. Most clubs provide parking facilities, though weekend spaces fill quickly.

For visitors staying outside C-Scheme, budget ₹200-400 ($2.50-5) for round-trip auto-rickshaw rides from popular areas like Pink City or Civil Lines. The area connects well with other [major attractions]((/jaipur/)), making it convenient for tourists.

## Timing and Best Days

Clubs operate from 8 PM to 1:30 AM, with last entry typically at midnight. Thursday nights offer the best balance of atmosphere and affordability, avoiding weekend premiums while maintaining decent crowds. Saturday nights provide the most energetic scenes but expect higher costs and longer waits.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is the entry fee for clubs in Jaipur?**

Entry fees range from ₹500-1500 ($6-18) depending on the venue and day. Weekend rates are higher, with premium clubs like Blackout charging ₹1200-1500 on Saturdays. Weekdays often have reduced or no entry fees.

**What is the age limit for pubs and clubs in Jaipur?**

All pubs and clubs in Jaipur enforce a strict 21+ age policy as per Rajasthan state laws. You must carry valid photo ID (Aadhaar, passport, or driver's license) for entry verification.

**Which area has the best nightlife in Jaipur?**

C-Scheme is Jaipur's nightlife hub, housing popular venues like Blackout, F Bar & Lounge, and Grunge. The area is well-connected and safe, with most clubs within walking distance of each other.
