**Mumbai's club scene centers around Trilogy in Juhu, Kitty Su at The Lalit, and 1Above, with cover charges ranging ₹2,000-5,000 ($24-60), smart casual dress codes, and peak hours from 11pm-3am.**

Mumbai's nightlife pulses strongest in its exclusive clubs, where Bollywood celebrities mix with business executives and international DJs spin until dawn. The city's club culture demands preparation - guest list entries, proper attire, and substantial cover charges are standard.

## Top Mumbai Nightclubs

### Trilogy, Juhu
Located at JW Marriott Mumbai Juhu, Trilogy remains Mumbai's most exclusive club. The three-level venue features different music zones - house and techno upstairs, commercial Bollywood on the main floor.

**Entry Details:**
- Cover charge: ₹4,000-6,000 ($48-72) depending on events
- Guest list essential for entry
- Strict dress code: no sneakers, shorts, or casual shirts
- Peak timing: 11:30pm-3am

### Kitty Su at The Lalit
This award-winning club at The Lalit Mumbai brings international DJs and cutting-edge sound systems. Known for electronic music and progressive house nights.

**Entry Details:**
- Cover charge: ₹3,500-5,000 ($42-60)
- Couples entry preferred
- Smart casual dress code enforced
- Best nights: Thursday-Saturday

### 1Above
Positioned as Mumbai's highest nightclub, 1Above offers panoramic city views alongside commercial music and Bollywood hits.

**Entry Details:**
- Cover charge: ₹2,500-3,500 ($30-42)
- More relaxed entry policy
- Smart casual attire required
- Popular with younger crowds

## Entry Procedures and Requirements

Getting into Mumbai's top clubs requires advance planning. Most venues maintain guest lists managed through their social media pages or dedicated WhatsApp numbers. Contact clubs directly 2-3 days before your visit.

**Standard Requirements:**
- Valid photo ID (passport for tourists)
- Couples or mixed groups preferred
- Advance reservation recommended
- Cover charges include limited drinks

Bouncers strictly enforce dress codes. Men need collared shirts, dress shoes, and full-length trousers. Women should avoid flip-flops and overly casual clothing. Designer labels help at premium venues.

## Music and Atmosphere

Mumbai clubs cater to diverse musical tastes:

**Electronic/House:** Trilogy and Kitty Su feature international DJs spinning progressive house, techno, and deep house. These venues attract serious music enthusiasts and industry professionals.

**Commercial/Bollywood:** 1Above and most mainstream clubs play popular Bollywood tracks, commercial house, and international chart-toppers. These venues focus on dancing and social atmosphere.

**Hip-Hop/Urban:** Several clubs dedicate specific nights to hip-hop and urban music, particularly targeting younger demographics.

## Practical Club Information

### Timing Strategy
Arrive by 10:30pm to avoid peak-hour queues. Clubs typically open at 9pm but remain quiet until 11pm. The energy peaks between midnight and 2am, when dance floors fill completely.

### Budget Planning
Beyond cover charges, expect to spend ₹1,500-3,000 ($18-36) per person on additional drinks. Premium spirits cost ₹800-1,200 ($10-14) per drink. Most clubs offer bottle service starting at ₹8,000 ($96) for standard brands.

### Transportation
Book return transportation before heading out. Local trains stop running after 12:30am, and taxis become scarce after 2am. Use app-based rides or arrange hotel pickup if staying in [areas covered in our Mumbai transport guide](/mumbai/transport/).

## Safety and Etiquette

Mumbai's club scene is generally safe, especially at high-end venues with professional security. However, follow basic precautions outlined in our [Mumbai safety guide](/mumbai/is-mumbai-safe-for-tourists/). Keep valuables secure, stay with your group, and avoid excessive drinking.

Club etiquette emphasizes respect and sophistication. Don't hassle other guests, follow staff instructions, and tip bartenders ₹100-200 ($1-2) for good service.

The best clubs integrate seamlessly into Mumbai's luxury hospitality scene. When planning your [Mumbai itinerary](/mumbai/how-many-days-mumbai/), reserve 1-2 nights for experiencing the city's renowned nightlife culture.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the cover charges for Mumbai clubs?
Cover charges range from ₹2,000-5,000 ($24-60). Premium clubs like Kitty Su charge ₹4,000-5,000, while places like 1Above start at ₹2,500. Prices increase on weekends and special events.

### What's the dress code for Mumbai nightclubs?
Smart casual is standard - collared shirts, dress shoes, and trousers for men. No flip-flops, shorts, or tank tops. Women should avoid overly casual attire. Designer clubs have stricter requirements.

### What time do Mumbai clubs get busy?
Peak hours are 11pm-3am. Arrive by 10:30pm to avoid long queues. Most clubs stay open until 3am on weekdays, 4am on weekends, though some have extended hours until 6am.
