**Mumbai's best bars range from sky-high rooftops like Aer (₹2,000+ cover) to quirky spots like Toto's Garage in Bandra, historic hotel bars like Harbour Bar at Taj, and trendy Social outlets across the city.**

Mumbai's bar scene spans everything from heritage hotel lounges to craft cocktail speakeasies and rooftop venues with stunning city views. You'll find options for every budget and vibe, whether you want artisanal cocktails or casual drinks with friends.

## Premium Rooftop Bars

**Aer** at Four Seasons (Worli) sits on the 34th floor with 360-degree city views. The cover charge starts at ₹2,000 ($24) on weekends, with signature cocktails priced ₹1,200-1,800 ($14-22). Their Mumbai Mule and Aviation cocktail are standouts.

**AER Bar & Lounge** in Lower Parel offers Marine Drive views from the 30th floor. Weekend covers run ₹1,500 ($18), and they're known for their extensive gin menu. Happy hour runs 4-8 PM weekdays with 1+1 cocktails.

**Skyye** at Upper Deck in Colaba provides rooftop dining with harbor views. No cover charge, but cocktails start at ₹900 ($11). Their terrace gets packed after 9 PM on weekends.

## Heritage Hotel Bars

**Harbour Bar** at Taj Mahal Palace (Colaba) has operated since 1933. This wood-paneled bar serves classic cocktails (₹800-1,200/$10-14) and is famous for its Old Fashioned. No cover charge, but expect a dress code.

**Shamiana** at Taj Mahal Palace offers a more casual hotel bar experience with live music on weekends. Cocktails range ₹700-1,000 ($8-12).

**Serafina** at The St. Regis (Lower Parel) combines Italian cuisine with creative cocktails. Their Negroni variations are excellent, priced ₹800-1,200 ($10-14).

## Trendy Chain Bars

**Social** has multiple locations (Khar, BKC, Hauz Khas) with industrial décor and board games. Covers range ₹800-1,200 ($10-14) depending on location and day. Their LIIT (Long Island Iced Tea) is popular at ₹450 ($5).

**The Bombay Canteen** in Mills Compound serves Indian-inspired cocktails like their Desi Daiquiri (₹550/$7). No cover charge, but reservations recommended.

## Bandra Hotspots

**Toto's Garage** on 30th Road is an auto-themed bar with vintage car décor. Cocktails range ₹400-700 ($5-8), and they don't charge covers. Their Toto's Special is a crowd favorite.

**Monkey Bar** in Bandra West offers Asian tapas with creative cocktails. Weekend covers are ₹1,000 ($12), with signature drinks priced ₹600-900 ($7-11).

**Trilogy** combines three concepts under one roof - a lounge, bar, and club. Entry charges vary ₹1,200-2,000 ($14-24) based on events.

## Craft Beer Scene

**The Barking Deer** in Bandra serves craft beers on tap (₹350-450/$4-5) alongside cocktails. Their IPA and wheat beer are locally brewed.

**Toit** has locations in Indiranagar and Koramangala, famous for craft beer. Pints cost ₹300-400 ($4-5).

**Gateway Taproom** at Gateway of India offers local craft beers with harbor views. Expect ₹400-500 ($5-6) per pint.

## Getting Around

Use [Mumbai's transport system](/mumbai/transport/) to bar hop safely. The metro reaches key areas like BKC and Lower Parel, while taxis work best for Bandra and Colaba spots. Most premium bars validate parking.

For those planning extended [stays in Mumbai](/mumbai/where-to-stay-mumbai/), Bandra and Lower Parel offer proximity to multiple bar districts.

## Happy Hour Timings

Most bars run happy hours 4-8 PM on weekdays:
- Social: 1+1 cocktails until 8 PM
- AER: 25% off all drinks 4-8 PM
- Harbour Bar: Buy 2 get 1 free 6-9 PM (Tuesday-Thursday)
- Monkey Bar: 1+1 on house cocktails until 7 PM

Weekend happy hours are rare, with most premium venues charging full prices Friday-Sunday.

## Safety and Etiquette

Mumbai bars maintain good security, but check our guide on [Mumbai safety for tourists](/mumbai/is-mumbai-safe-for-tourists/) for general precautions. Most upscale venues enforce dress codes - smart casual minimum, no flip-flops or shorts after 8 PM.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What's the cover charge at Mumbai's premium bars?**

Premium bars like Aer charge ₹2,000+ ($24) covers, while mid-range spots like Social charge ₹800-1,200 ($10-14). Hotel bars typically have no cover but higher drink prices starting ₹800 ($10) per cocktail.

**Which bars have the best rooftop views in Mumbai?**

Aer at Four Seasons offers panoramic city views from the 34th floor. AER Bar & Lounge in Lower Parel and Skyye at Upper Deck provide excellent Marine Drive and skyline views with outdoor seating.

**When is happy hour at Mumbai bars?**

Most bars offer happy hour 4-8 PM on weekdays with 1+1 offers or 25-50% discounts. Social runs deals until 8 PM, while hotel bars like Harbour Bar offer evening promotions 6-9 PM Tuesday-Thursday.
