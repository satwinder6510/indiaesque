Mumbai parties harder than anywhere else in India. Here's your guide to the city after dark.

---

## The Scene

Mumbai nightlife is diverse and sophisticated. The city has rooftop bars with Arabian Sea views, underground clubs, craft breweries, jazz venues, and bars open until the early hours.

**Legal drinking age:** 25 in Maharashtra (enforced at upscale venues)
**Closing time:** 1:30 AM officially (some venues have later permits)

---

## Best Bars by Area

### Colaba

Tourist-friendly area with accessible bars.

**Leopold Cafe**
Iconic since 1871. Tourist institution. Cold beer, casual food. Historic (survived 2008 attacks).

**Woodside Inn**
Cozy pub atmosphere. Good beer selection. Burgers. No pretense.

**Cafe Mondegar**
Art on walls, jukebox vibes. Beer and continental food.

**Gateway Taproom**
Craft brewery. Fresh beers, good food.

### Bandra

The hip neighbourhood. Where Mumbai's creatives drink.

**Toto's Garage**
Rock bar institution. Live music, cheap drinks, grungy. A Mumbai classic.

**The Barking Deer**
India's first craft brewery. Excellent beers brewed on-site.

**Pali Village Cafe**
All-day cafe that turns into drinks in the evening.

**Monkey Bar**
Fun vibe, gastropub food, cocktails. Quirky interiors.

**Slink & Bardot**
Speakeasy style. Great cocktails. Hidden entrance.

### Lower Parel

The corporate-turned-nightlife district. Mills converted to venues.

**Aer (Four Seasons)**
Rooftop bar. 34th floor. Stunning views. Expensive but worth it for the sunset.

**Social**
Multi-floor creative space. Affordable. Always busy.

**Bonobo**
Underground vibes. Electronic music. Art crowd.

## Clubs

### Commercial/Mainstream

**Kitty Su (The LaLit)**
Biggest names, international DJs. Expensive. Lower Parel.

**Tryst**
Popular with young crowd. Commercial music. Lower Parel.

**Trilogy**
Juhu location. Sea-facing. Popular with Bollywood crowd.

### Alternative/Underground

**Bonobo**
Electronic music, alternative scene. Lower Parel.

**Toto's Garage**
Rock and indie. Bandra. Live music nights.

**antiSOCIAL**
Live music, indie events. Khar.

## What to Expect

### Dress Code

**Rooftop bars:** Smart casual
**Clubs:** No shorts, no flip-flops (men)
**Casual bars:** Anything presentable

### Entry

**Cover charges:** ₹1,000-3,000 at clubs (sometimes redeemable)
**Guest lists:** For popular clubs, get on the list via promoters

### Prices

| Drink | Range |
|-------|-------|
| Domestic beer | ₹200-400 |
| Imported beer | ₹400-700 |
| Cocktail | ₹500-900 |
| At clubs | 50-100% markup |

### Couples Policy

Some clubs prefer couples. Single men may face higher covers or denial. Less strict than Delhi but exists.

## Night Safety

Mumbai is relatively safe at night. Tips:

- Use Uber/Ola home
- Colaba, Bandra, Lower Parel are fine areas
- Share location with someone
- Don't drink and drive (police checkpoints)

See our [safety guide](/mumbai/safety-guide/) for details.
