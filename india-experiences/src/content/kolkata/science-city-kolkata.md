**Science City Kolkata is the largest science center in the Indian subcontinent, featuring interactive exhibits, 3D theaters, and Space Odyssey experiences. Entry fees range from ₹30-200 ($0.35-2.40) per section, and you need a full day to explore everything.**

Located in the Eastern Metropolitan Bypass area, Science City spans 50 acres and attracts over 2 million visitors annually. This isn't just another museum - it's an interactive playground where science comes alive through hands-on exhibits, immersive theaters, and cutting-edge technology.

## Entry Fees and Ticketing

Science City operates on a sectional ticketing system, allowing you to choose specific attractions:

**Basic Entry Fees:**
- General admission: ₹30 ($0.35)
- Space Odyssey: ₹200 ($2.40)
- 3D Vision Theater: ₹100 ($1.20)
- Dynamotion Hall: ₹150 ($1.80)
- Time Machine: ₹100 ($1.20)
- Evolution Park: ₹50 ($0.60)

Children under 3 enter free, while students with valid ID cards get 50% discounts. The combo tickets covering multiple sections offer better value - a full-experience ticket costs around ₹500 ($6) per person.

Book tickets online at the official Science City website to avoid queues, especially during weekends and school holidays. Online booking opens 7 days in advance, and you can print e-tickets or show them on your mobile device.

## Major Attractions

### Space Odyssey
The crown jewel of Science City, Space Odyssey takes you on a simulated journey through the solar system. This 25-minute experience uses advanced projection technology and motion simulators to create an authentic space travel feeling. Shows run every hour from 10:30 AM to 5:30 PM, with separate Hindi and English sessions.

### 3D Vision Theater
The theater screens educational documentaries about marine life, prehistoric animals, and space exploration. The 45-minute shows use polarized 3D technology with comfortable seating for 100 people. Popular shows include "Ocean Wonderland" and "Dinosaurs Alive."

### Dynamotion Hall
This interactive physics gallery demonstrates scientific principles through hands-on exhibits. You can experience optical illusions, generate electricity using pedal power, and understand sound waves through musical instruments. The hall stays open throughout operating hours with no time restrictions.

### Evolution Park
An outdoor exhibit showcasing the evolution of life on Earth through life-sized dinosaur models and prehistoric landscapes. The 2-acre park includes a fossil gallery and interactive displays explaining Darwin's theory of evolution.

## Planning Your Visit

### Timing and Duration
Science City opens at 9 AM and closes at 8 PM (Tuesday to Sunday). Monday is maintenance day, so the center remains closed. Plan to spend 6-8 hours to cover all major attractions comfortably.

Start your visit early to avoid afternoon crowds. The [best time to visit Kolkata](/kolkata/best-time-visit-kolkata/) is October to March when weather conditions are ideal for outdoor sections like Evolution Park.

### Getting There
Science City is located at JBS Haldane Avenue, near the Eastern Metropolitan Bypass. The easiest way to reach is by taxi or app-based cab from central Kolkata (₹150-200 or $1.80-2.40).

If you're using public transport, take the Metro to Karunamoyee Station on the East-West line, then hire an auto-rickshaw for the remaining 3 km (₹40-50 or $0.50-0.60). For detailed transport options, check our [Kolkata transport guide](/kolkata/kolkata-transport-guide/).

### Parking and Amenities
The complex provides ample parking space for cars (₹20 or $0.25) and two-wheelers (₹10 or $0.12). The parking area is well-lit and secured with CCTV surveillance.

Multiple food courts serve Indian, Chinese, and Continental cuisine. Prices range from ₹80-250 ($1-3) per meal. You can also bring packed food - several picnic areas are available within the campus.

Clean restrooms, first aid facilities, and wheelchairs for disabled visitors are available free of charge. ATMs and souvenir shops operate near the main entrance.

## Why Families Love Science City

Science City excels as a family destination because it makes learning fun for children while keeping parents engaged. The interactive exhibits encourage kids to touch, experiment, and discover rather than just observe.

The Aquarium section houses over 40 species of fish, including exotic marine life. Children can participate in science workshops conducted every weekend, covering topics like robotics, astronomy, and environmental science.

Safety measures are excellent - all equipment undergoes regular maintenance, and trained staff supervise interactive areas. The wide pathways and clear signage make navigation easy for families with young children or elderly members.

When planning your [things to do in Kolkata](/kolkata/) itinerary, Science City works well as a full-day activity, especially if you're traveling with children. Unlike traditional museums, this hands-on approach keeps kids entertained while adults appreciate the scientific depth of exhibits.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much time do you need at Science City Kolkata?
Plan a full day (6-8 hours) to explore Science City properly. The complex has multiple sections, theaters, and interactive exhibits that require substantial time to experience fully.

### Can you book Science City tickets online in advance?
Yes, you can book tickets online through the official Science City website. Advance booking is recommended, especially on weekends and holidays when crowds are larger.

### What are the entry fees for different sections at Science City?
Entry fees range from ₹30-200 ($0.35-2.40) depending on sections. Basic entry is ₹30, while premium experiences like 3D theaters and Space Odyssey cost ₹100-200 each.
