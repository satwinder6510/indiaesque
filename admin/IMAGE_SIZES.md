# Image Sizes Required

## Cities

Each city needs these images:

| Image | Size | Aspect | Use |
|-------|------|--------|-----|
| `{city}-hero.jpg` | 1200×600 | 2:1 | City page hero |
| `{city}-hero-mobile.jpg` | 800×600 | 4:3 | City page hero (mobile) |
| `{city}-card.jpg` | 500×313 | 16:10 | Home page city cards |
| `{city}-list.jpg` | 800×500 | 16:10 | Destinations landing page |

**Location:** `/india-experiences/public/images/cities/`

---

## Naming Convention

- Use lowercase, hyphens for spaces
- No special characters
- Format: `{category}/{name}-{variant}.jpg`

Examples:
- `cities/delhi-hero.jpg`
- `cities/delhi-card.jpg`
- `staycations/rajasthan-palace-portrait.jpg`
- `experiences/food-tours-card.jpg`
